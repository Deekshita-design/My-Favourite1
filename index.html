Hermione Jean Granger

About

Hermione Jean Granger is a fictional character who was born to Mr. and Mrs. Granger, two muggle dentists was the best friend of Harry Potter and Ron Weasley. As her parents were muggles she did not know she was a witch until she was almost twelve. Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger had many fun but mostly dangerous adventures during their time at Hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry. She was alwas the first in her class. At present Hermione works as minister of magic, married to Ron Weasley with two kids Hugo and Rose Granger Weasley

Life
Personality
Hermione Granger is one of the three main characters in the Harry Potter Movie Series. Played by Emma Watson, Hermione was always the studious and most book smart of the group. Each school year, the group were always part of a dangerous and adventurous quest. As she spent more time with Harry and Ron, she became more adventurous and daring. In her seventh year, Hermione and her friends journeyed on a quest to defeat Voldemort. After defeating Voldemort, Hermione went back to school to study further and completed her seventh year at Hogwarts. She first obtained a job in the Department of Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures. She continued to be an advocate for the rights of non humans such as house elves. Later, she became the deputy head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement.
